About TCC

Tranz Central Coast (TCC) is a grassroots, volunteer-driven organization dedicated to creating safe and affirming spaces for transgender, nonbinary, and gender-diverse people. We bring our community together through support groups, social events, and educational outreach while connecting individuals and families with inclusive local resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support, uplift, and celebrate the transgender and gender-diverse community of the Central Coast by fostering connection, building resilience, and advancing equity through advocacy and education.

Who We Serve

TCC serves transgender, nonbinary, and gender-diverse individuals of all ages, as well as their families, friends, and allies. Our reach extends across California’s Central Coast, including San Luis Obispo and Santa Barbara counties, where we work to strengthen visibility, provide affirming support, and build bridges between community members and inclusive local organizations.

Leadership

TCC is guided by a volunteer leadership team committed to supporting our community with compassion, inclusivity, and collaboration. Our leaders bring diverse experience and work together to ensure that programs and resources remain accessible and relevant.

Committee Chair: Landon St. James (he/him)

Tranz Central Coast Committee Chair

Landon enjoys life on the Central Coast with his beautiful wife, 3 kids, 3 dogs, 1 cat (and a few other barnyard animals). He’s proud to advocate for the queer community, especially our trans youth. Landon loves to build community – relationships, resources, safety structures, and has found so much fulfillment serving trans and gender non-conforming people through Tranz Central Coast. He decided to get involved with TCC because so often when meeting other local trans people, they would tell him he was the first trans person (besides themselves) they had met. “We need to find ways to connect with each other!” If you see him around town or at a TCC event please stop him and say hello.
