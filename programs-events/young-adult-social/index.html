Trans* Young Adult Social Group

When 2nd Saturday of each month, 2pm – 4pm
Where Varies. Sometimes at the GALA Pride & Diversity Center, other times at parks, businesses, or community events. Email us for details: tcc@galacc.org
Ages 18 – 40

This is a social group for transgender and nonbinary people, ages 18–40. It isn’t a support group—it’s a place to get together, do something, meet new people, and have fun!
