Parent Support Group

UPDATE: Our Parent Support Group is currently on hold while we train new facilitators. Please check back for updates.

When 2nd Tuesday of each month, 6:15pm – 7:45pm
Where If you are interested in attending once the group resumes, please email us at tcc@galacc.org/

The “Stand Out” LGBTQ+ parent/family support group meets alongside our Trans Youth Support Group. Parents can drop their kids off at the GALA Center and then connect with other parents to discuss common issues. The group is open to the parents of all lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, nonbinary, and questioning children.
