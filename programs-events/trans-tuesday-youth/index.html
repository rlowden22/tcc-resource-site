Trans* Tuesday Adult Support Group

When Every Tuesday, 7pm – 9pm
Where GALA Pride and Diversity Center, 1060 Palm St, SLO
Ages 18+
Contact Email TCC at tcc@galacc.org

Trans* Tuesdays Adult Support Group meets weekly on Tuesdays. We are a diverse group with gender identities ranging along the full gender spectrum: trans men and women, trans femme and masc, non-binary, gender nonconforming, gender queer, asexual, and questioning. Our group ranges from 18 years old to senior citizens. All people who are questioning their gender identities are welcome.

We have fun, often insightful, and very informative discussions. Trans* Tuesdays is a facilitated emotional support group. It is a safe place to be heard, to share your story, and to hear stories that may sound surprisingly like your own. Join us!
