Glossary

Plain-language definitions for terms used across this site. This page is for general information and is not legal or medical advice.

A C H I N P S

A

Accessibility
Designing services and spaces so people with disabilities can use them equitably (e.g., ramps, captions, readable websites).

C

Case Manager
A trained professional who helps coordinate services (healthcare, housing, benefits) and advocates on your behalf.

H

Housing Navigator
Someone who assists with finding housing options, applications, and understanding eligibility requirements.

I

Insurance Navigator
A person or program that explains health insurance options and can help you enroll in coverage.

N

Name Change (Legal)
The official court process to change your legal name. Steps can include forms, filing fees, publication, and updated IDs.

P

Primary Care
Your main healthcare provider for routine care, referrals, and preventative services.

S

Sliding Scale
A payment model where fees are adjusted based on your income to make services more affordable.
Support Group
A facilitated meeting where people with shared experiences connect, share, and learn together.
